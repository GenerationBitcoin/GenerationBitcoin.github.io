What is Generation Bitcoin?

Generation Bitcoin is a place to help teenagers start learning about Bitcoin through group based discussions, projects, readings, etc. We want to provide a safe, welcoming environment for people to grow and experiments with Bitcoin.

What Resources Do We Use?

We use a varity of resources with differing degrees of difficulty to accommodate different types of learners. We are open for suggestions, so feel free to leave a PR on our Github.
