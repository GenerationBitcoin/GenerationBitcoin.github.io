Frequently Asked Questions

We are an organization designed for teenagers (ages 13-19).

You can add helpful resources to our Resources page by making a pull request on GitHub or DM’ing us on Twitter. You can also DM us if you would be interested in being a guest speaker!

It’s totally okay to join GenB if you’re not familiar with Bitcoin. Everyone starts somewhere! We’d recommend that you check out some of the beginner resources listed on the Resources page as these have helped out our members initially learn about Bitcoin as well.

Generation Bitcoin is, as the name implies, a Bitcoin-only group focused on helping teens get into this space.
